User Research

My typical approach is to start with direct one-on-one interviews, targeting users that fit the correct demographic. I then create survey questions which allows me to reach out to a larger group of people to pull quantitative data.

User Empathy

When conducting interviews, I excel at putting my biased opinion behind me. I must really listen to the users questions and answers to dive deeper into the universal problem my interviewees share.

Qualitative Interviews

When interviewing, I always be sure to record the session with the users approval. Take notes and observe the users actions. Valuable information can be harnessed using these methods.
